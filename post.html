Single Post of CHIRAG

June 16, 2020 posted by NADEEM

CBT is a type of psychotherapy (“talking therapy”). It stands for Cognitive Behaviour Therapy. Cognitive refers to thoughts and Behaviour refers to actions. So we can state that Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) is like a talking therapy used to treat people with a wide range of mental health problems and is based on how we think and how we act together. It was developed by Aaron T. Beck in the 1960s. He initially developed it for treating depression ,later on it was expanded to include behavioural components and became CBT. Since then it has been successfully used for treating a range of diseases on its own or at times combined with medication like anxiety, depression ,eating disorders etc. CBT sessions are goal oriented and focused in which patients work with their therapist towards mutually set goals. CBT can also be an effective tool to help anyone(doesn’t necessarily mean that he or she should have a disorder) learn how to better manage stressful life situations. Many mental health chatbots have been recently created like Woebot, Wysa etc which uses CBT as its core principle for treatment with sessions being taken by the chatbot each having few features implemented in a better way than the other which is discussed it the later section . Still this has and will continue to face many challenges as here basically a chatbot(AI) is trying to take up the role of a therapist(human). Cognitive behavioral therapy may not cure the patient’s condition in all cases , but it can give strength to cope up with situation in a healthy way to feel better and for this very reason it has become increasingly popular in recent years with mental health consumers as it’s also a convenient and an affordable way of treatment. This study on CBT explains and analyses mechanism to treat mental health patients which we are using in our chatbot.
